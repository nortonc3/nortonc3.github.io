The Use of Technology in Healthcare

URL What is Healthcare Technology? Applications and Benefits
Site Author Jim Hearson
Abstract This site covers all things healthcare technology. It begins with an overview of technology in healthcare, briefly discussing its evolution and many benefits among other things. The site then talks about the types of healthcare technology, including artificial intelligence (AI), blockchain, cloud technology, telemedicine, remote monitoring, and interoperability. 5 applications of healthcare technology are also mentioned. They include administrative health tech (such as some connected to EHRs), surgical tech like robotic arms and 3D printing, drug development, fitness wearables like smart watches and bracelets, and diagnostic systems which reduce medical errors. It ends by discussing future trends regarding technology in medicine.

URL 6 Pros of Technology in Healthcare
Site Author Rachna Kumar
Abstract As the title suggests, this site discusses 6 benefits of technology in healthcare. First is enhanced patient care and monitoring. It discusses wearable technology and telemedicine, and how advancements like these aid virtual patient care. Next is increased accessibility to healthcare services. The author mentions how some people have limited access to services, and mentions that new technology works to fix this. Third is improved efficiency and workflow. New technology can help gather patient information, streamline billing, and improve mobility within the medical field. Then there's innovations in treatment and diagnosis, including 3D printing, implantable artificial organs, and robotic surgeries. Fifth is decision making driven by data and facts, and last is reduced healthcare costs. Overall, this is a great site covering all the ways technology is changing healthcare.
